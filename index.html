Great coffee
for some joy

Love Brews is a coffee company that believes in the power of love and great coffee. We use only the highest quality coffee beans to craft our signature blends, and every cup is brewed with love. Our mission is to create a warm and welcoming environment where our customers can relax, connect with others, and enjoy a delicious cup of coffee. We believe that coffee has the power to bring people together, and we're committed to creating a positive impact in our community through our love of coffee.

Our story

Our coffee bar uses only Ethiopian coffee. All commercially produced coffee originates from Ethiopia. We love Ethiopia for providing the world with coffee to grow, and we continue to love the flavor of that country’s coffee. The fruity acidity of this single origin coffee is balanced by its sweet honey notes, creating soft, light notes with a light character.
